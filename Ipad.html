The New iPad Models

Explore the latest iPads from Apple

iPad Pro 12.9-inch / 11-inch (M2)

The iPad Pro models feature the powerful M2 chip, stunning Liquid Retina displays, and support for Apple Pencil hover. They offer advanced camera systems, Thunderbolt connectivity, and are perfect for creative professionals and power users.

iPad Air (5th Gen)

The iPad Air (5th Gen) is powered by the M1 chip, offering fast performance and great graphics. It features a 10.9-inch Liquid Retina display, 5G connectivity, and comes in five beautiful colors.

iPad (10th Gen)

The iPad (10th Gen) features a 10.9-inch Liquid Retina display, A14 Bionic chip, and USB-C connectivity. It supports the Magic Keyboard Folio and comes in four vibrant colors, making it a versatile choice for everyday use.

iPad mini (6th Gen)

The iPad mini (6th Gen) packs a powerful A15 Bionic chip into a compact 8.3-inch design. It supports Apple Pencil (2nd Gen), has USB-C, and is perfect for portability and one-handed use.
